Kepler 309 c

Kepler 309 c is a planet similar to Neptune and orbits a K-type star. 105.4 days are necessary for this planet to orbit
its star and it has a mass 6.85 times that of Earth. Kepler 309 c is a 0401 AU from the star
that it orbits plus in 2014, its founding was announced.
